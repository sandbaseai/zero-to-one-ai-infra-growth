# 30-Day Growth Diary

Part 1 of Global AI Cold Start.

This is the daily build log for turning SandBase from "a product exists" into "the market can see and trust it".

It records how an AI infrastructure startup builds its public growth foundation:

- SEO and crawlability
- technical blog infrastructure
- X, LinkedIn, Discord, GitHub, and Dev.to
- developer directories and backlinks
- daily social/community operations
- lessons from using Codex as an AI growth partner

What to copy:

- how to set up the first public trust layer
- what to do before submitting directories
- how to turn social posts into technical positioning
- how to publish off-site content without hurting SEO
- how to record daily growth work so it compounds
