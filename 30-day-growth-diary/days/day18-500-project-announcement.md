## What We Learned

- A strong announcement needs three parts:
  - a useful open-source link
  - a visual that explains the category map
  - a correction/submission path for maintainers
- The safest outreach pattern is still correction-oriented: ask maintainers whether the category or description is accurate, rather than asking for promotion.
