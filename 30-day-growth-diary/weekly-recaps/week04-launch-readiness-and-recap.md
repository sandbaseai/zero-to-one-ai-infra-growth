# Week 4 Plan — Launch Readiness And 30-Day Recap

Date range: Day 22 to Day 30

Stage: content distribution, trust-surface cleanup, launch prep, and final case study

## Goal

Week 4 closes the first cold-start cycle.

The objective is not to create a noisy launch. The objective is to make every public SandBase surface consistent, useful, and ready for the next phase: developer adoption.

```text
Finish the trust system.
Record what worked.
Turn 30 days of operations into a reusable public case study.
```

## Weekly Outcomes

By the end of Week 4, we want:

- LinkedIn company page with a clear B2B infrastructure narrative
- external profiles cleaned up and recorded
- website trust surfaces checked
- Product Hunt or launch-channel assets prepared, even if launch waits
- community submission loop for open-source projects started
- metrics and learnings collected
- final 30-day recap drafted or published
- next 30-day operating theme selected
