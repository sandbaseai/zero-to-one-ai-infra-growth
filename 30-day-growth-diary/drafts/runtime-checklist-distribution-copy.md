## LinkedIn

```text
We published a practical checklist for evaluating AI agent sandbox runtimes.

As agents move from demos into production, "sandboxed" needs to mean more than a vague security label. Operators need runtime behavior they can inspect:

- capability discovery before execution
- filesystem and network boundaries during execution
- timeout, cancellation, and cleanup behavior
- audit and trace output after failures or denied actions
- integration surfaces that frameworks can call reliably

The short version:

What can this runtime prove before execution?
What can it enforce during execution?
What can it explain after execution?
```
